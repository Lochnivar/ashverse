# Lore & History

## Creation Myth
The world was born from the First Flame, which burned until only ash remained. From the ash, life emerged, and the first civilizations rose. The Ash Empire was said to be the closest to understanding the original flame, which is why their fall was so catastrophic.

## Major Historical Events

### The Rise of the Ash Empire
- **When:** Approximately 2,000 years ago
- **What Happened:** A powerful civilization rose to dominance, mastering magic and technology beyond what exists today
- **Significance:** Created the foundation for modern understanding of magic and the world
- **Impact:** Established trade routes, built great cities, advanced knowledge

### The Great Burning
- **When:** Exactly 1,000 years ago (the current year is 1000 AB - After Burning)
- **What Happened:** The entire Ash Empire was consumed in a catastrophic event. Official records claim it was a magical accident or hubris, but the truth is unknown. The empire's heartland became the Ashen Wastes.
- **Significance:** The defining event of modern history, everything is measured from this point
- **Impact:** 
  - Destruction of the most advanced civilization
  - Loss of vast knowledge
  - Creation of the Ashen Wastes
  - Birth of the first Ashborn (those who survived and were changed)
  - Scattering of survivors who founded new kingdoms

### The Scattering
- **When:** Immediately after the Great Burning
- **What Happened:** Survivors fled the destruction, founding new settlements and eventually kingdoms
- **Significance:** Led to the current political structure
- **Impact:** Created the modern kingdoms, established new cultures, but also led to loss of knowledge

### The First Ashborn Persecution
- **When:** 200 years after the Great Burning
- **What Happened:** Fear of Ashborn power led to widespread persecution and hunting
- **Significance:** Established the pattern of fear and hatred that continues today
- **Impact:** Most Ashborn went into hiding, knowledge was further lost

## Legends & Myths

### The Last Emperor
Legend says the final emperor of the Ash Empire made a terrible choice that led to the Great Burning. Some say he tried to achieve godhood, others that he sought to prevent a greater disaster. His fate is unknown.

### The Phoenix Prophecy
An ancient prophecy (possibly from before the empire) speaks of a "phoenix from the ash" who will either restore what was lost or bring final destruction. Many interpret this as referring to a powerful Ashborn.

### The Hidden Vaults
Legend claims that before the fall, the empire hid their greatest treasures and knowledge in secret vaults throughout the wastes. Many have searched, few have returned.

## Religions & Beliefs

### The Order of Remembrance
- **Deities/Figures:** The First Flame (creator), the Last Emperor (fallen figure)
- **Core Beliefs:** Honor the past, learn from mistakes, preserve memory
- **Practices:** Ritual burning of offerings, keeping records, memorial services
- **Followers:** Scholars, historians, some common folk

### The Purifiers
- **Deities/Figures:** The Cleansing Fire (destructive force that purifies)
- **Core Beliefs:** The Great Burning was necessary, Ashborn are abominations, the past must be forgotten
- **Practices:** Hunt Ashborn, destroy artifacts, burn records of the empire
- **Followers:** Fanatics, some nobles, the Ashen Hunters

### The Way of Balance
- **Deities/Figures:** Natural forces, the cycle of creation and destruction
- **Core Beliefs:** Everything has its place, balance must be maintained
- **Practices:** Meditation, respect for all things, avoiding extremes
- **Followers:** Mystics, some isolated communities

## Prophecies

### The Phoenix Prophecy
*"When the ash stirs and the forgotten wake,  
A phoenix shall rise from the embers' break.  
With silver eyes and power old,  
The choice is made: destroy or hold.  
The past returns, the future bends,  
On this one choice, all depends."*

Interpretation varies, but most agree it refers to a powerful Ashborn who will face a crucial decision.

## Ancient Artifacts

### The Awakening Stone
The artifact that awakened Kaelen. A palm-sized crystal that glows when near Ashborn. Purpose unknown, but clearly significant.

### The Emperor's Crown
Lost in the Great Burning. Said to grant immense power but also terrible responsibility. Many have searched for it.

### The Memory Crystals
Crystals that store memories and experiences from the old empire. Extremely rare and valuable to scholars.

### The Ashen Keys
Artifacts that can unlock certain ruins or vaults. Their locations are unknown.

## Lost Knowledge
- The true cause of the Great Burning
- Advanced magical techniques of the empire
- The location of hidden vaults and sanctuaries
- The purpose of many artifacts
- The complete history of the empire's final days
- Methods of creating or controlling Ashborn
- The original language and writing of the empire (partially preserved)

## Notes
- Official records are incomplete and may be deliberately altered
- The truth about the Great Burning is the central mystery of the series
- Many believe the empire's fall was not an accident
- Some claim to have seen visions or received messages from the past
