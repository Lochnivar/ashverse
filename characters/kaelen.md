# Character: Kaelen

## Basic Information
- **Full Name:** Kaelen Ashworth
- **Age:** 19
- **Gender/Pronouns:** Male, he/him
- **Species/Race:** Human (Ashborn)
- **Occupation/Role:** Former apprentice blacksmith, now fugitive Ashborn

## Physical Description
- **Height:** 5'10"
- **Build:** Lean but strong from years of smithing
- **Hair:** Dark brown, often covered in ash and dust
- **Eyes:** Unusual gray-silver, a mark of his Ashborn heritage
- **Distinguishing Features:** A small scar on his left hand from his first encounter with an artifact; his eyes sometimes seem to glow faintly when using his power
- **Clothing Style:** Practical work clothes, leather apron (initially), later acquires travel gear

## Personality
- **Core Traits:** Curious, stubborn, protective, haunted by questions about his past
- **Strengths:** Quick learner, loyal to those he trusts, determined, good with his hands
- **Weaknesses:** Impulsive, struggles with self-doubt, tends to hide his emotions, naive about the wider world
- **Fears:** Losing control of his power, being captured, discovering something terrible about his parents
- **Desires:** To understand who he is, to protect those he cares about, to find his place in the world
- **Quirks:** Absentmindedly traces patterns in ash or dust when thinking; has a habit of examining objects closely

## Background
- **Birthplace:** Unknown (raised in Emberfall)
- **Family:** Parents disappeared/killed when he was young; raised by the town blacksmith, Master Corin
- **Education:** Basic reading/writing, extensive practical knowledge from blacksmithing
- **Key Life Events:** 
  - Parents' disappearance at age 7 (mysterious circumstances)
  - Taken in by Master Corin
  - Discovery of the artifact that awakened his powers
  - Forced flight from Emberfall

## Skills & Abilities
- Blacksmithing and metalwork
- Basic combat (improves throughout the story)
- Ashborn magic (newly awakened, raw and uncontrolled)
- Keen observation skills
- Physical strength and endurance

## Relationships
- **Master Corin:** Father figure, the blacksmith who raised him
- **Lyra:** Ally and friend, becomes his guide to understanding Ashborn powers
- **Thorne:** Complex relationship - initially distrustful, later develops mutual respect
- **Elder Mara:** Mentor figure who helps him understand his heritage

## Character Arc
- **Starting Point:** Content but restless, unaware of his true nature, living a simple life
- **Internal Conflict:** Fear of his own power vs. need to protect others; desire for answers vs. fear of what he might discover
- **Growth:** Learns to accept his power and responsibility, gains confidence, becomes a leader
- **Ending Point:** Embraces his Ashborn identity, ready to face the larger threat, but still has much to learn

## Voice & Dialogue
- **Speech Patterns:** Direct and honest, sometimes awkward in social situations, uses simple language
- **Catchphrases:** "I need to know" (when determined), "What does it mean?" (when confused)
- **Vocabulary:** Practical, working-class, becomes more sophisticated as he learns about the world

## Notes
- His silver-gray eyes are a rare but not unique trait among Ashborn
- The artifact that awakened him may have been left by his parents
- His blacksmithing background gives him unique insight into working with materials touched by ash-magic

## Appearances
- **Book 1:** Main protagonist, appears in all chapters
- **Book 2+:** Continues as main character
