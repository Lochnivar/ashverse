- Some presidents require longer lives than OTL
- Party transitions require economic justification
- Some ages are older/younger than ideal but plausible

**Individual:**
- Stephens: Early death (OTL died 1883, NTL dies 1871) - **Justification:** Health issues, stress
- Breckinridge: Longer life (+13 years) - **Justification:** Less stress, better health
- Hill: Longer life (+16 years), tongue cancer - **Justification:** Cancer caught early, treated

**Party Transitions:**
