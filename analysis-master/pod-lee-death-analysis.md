- Location: Headquarters tent at Widow Leister house
- Manner: Dies mid-sentence while dictating orders

- OTL: Lee survived Gettysburg, died 1870 (age 63)

- Must occur after battle of July 1 (no butterfly effects before)
- Must be clean, simple POD
