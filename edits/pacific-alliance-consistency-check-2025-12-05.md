## SPECIFIC ISSUES IDENTIFIED

### Issue 1: Oregon/Washington Sovereignty Status (1907) ✅ RESOLVED

**Problem:** Unclear whether Oregon/Washington are:
- Still U.S. states (like NATO membership)
- Independent nations (seceded from U.S.)

**Resolution (December 5, 2025):**
- **Official Status:** Oregon and Washington remain constituent states of the United States
- **Alliance Status:** Simultaneously become full founding members of the Pacific Alliance with delegated authority
- **Mechanism:** Enabling legislation (1906-1907) + Congressional ratification under Treaty Power (parallel to interstate compacts)
- **Scope:** Exclusive competence for tariffs, merchant shipping, naval coordination, and foreign relations affecting the Pacific Ocean

**Status:** ✅ **RESOLVED** - Official language added to `pacific-alliance-cascadia-compact.md`

### Issue 2: Bremerton Naval Station Arrangement ✅ RESOLVED

**Problem:** Bremerton is listed as "joint naval station" but:
- Bremerton is U.S. naval base (Puget Sound)
- How is it "joint" with Alliance if U.S. controls it?

**Resolution (December 5, 2025):**
- **Designation:** Permanent joint naval station of the Pacific Alliance and the United States Navy
- **Command Rotation:** Annual rotation between California Navy flag officer and U.S. Navy flag officer
- **Funding:** Costs apportioned by tonnage-days; California contributes 68-72%
- **Access Rights:** Unrestricted for Alliance vessels; equivalent rights for U.S. vessels via joint harbor master
- **Strategic Purpose:** Public (common defense) vs. Internal (prevents unilateral U.S. action north of San Francisco)

**Status:** ✅ **RESOLVED** - Official joint operating protocol added to `pacific-alliance-cascadia-compact.md`
