# California in the American Concord: The Younger Sibling Role

The Republic of California in the American Concord triad is the **youngest sibling** who moved out early, didn't get involved in the family politics, made it rich, and now shows up to family reunions in a Tesla wearing sunglasses indoors.

**United States** – the moralizing older brother who still thinks he's in charge because he used to be bigger

**Confederate States** – the proud middle brother who won his independence but knows he's still working through some baggage

**Republic of California** – the **youngest sibling** who moved out early, didn't get involved in the family politics, made it rich, and now owns half the Pacific, writes the checks, hosts the summits, and lets the other two posture while it actually runs the show

- The North **knows** they can be moralistic and preachy
- The South **knows** they still have internal issues they need to address
- California is the only one who ever shows up **prepared, solvent, and emotionally uninvolved**
