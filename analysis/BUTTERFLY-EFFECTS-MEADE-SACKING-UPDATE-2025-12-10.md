- Warren relieved: January 1864
- **Tenure:** ~2 months

**New Timeline:**

- Warren's relief is more justified (longer tenure = more failures)
- Warren's bitterness more understandable (5 months of failure vs. 2 months)

- Meade → Warren (for Rappahannock-Rapidan and Hagerstown)
- Warren's failures are more extensive (longer tenure = more failures)
- Lincoln's wisdom is more justified (learned from earlier panic mistake)
