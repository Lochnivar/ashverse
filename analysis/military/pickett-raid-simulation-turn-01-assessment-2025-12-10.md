### ⚠️ Issues Identified

**1. Movement Rates - Inconsistency:**

**Framework Specified:**
- Raiders: 25-35 miles/day (mounted march)
- Union Cavalry: 30-40 miles/day (matching raiders)

**Turn 1 Actual:**
- Raiders: 45-60 miles/day (exceeded framework)
- Union Cavalry: 30-35 miles/day (within framework)

**Problem:** Raiders moved faster than framework allowed. This needs correction.

**Correction Needed:**
- Raiders should move 25-35 miles/day (as per framework)
- Turn 1 should show: 75-105 miles in 3 days (25-35 mi/day)
- Current simulation showed: 145-175 miles (48-58 mi/day) - **TOO FAST**

**2. Distance Tracking:**

**Framework:** Raiders maintain lead through mobility advantage

**Turn 1 Result:** Union cavalry closing distance (40-60 miles behind)

**Assessment:** If raiders move 25-35 mi/day and Union cavalry moves 30-35 mi/day, the gap should be stable or slightly widening (if raiders started ahead). The closing distance suggests either:
- Raiders are moving slower than stated, OR
- Union cavalry is moving faster than stated, OR
- Initial head start was smaller than calculated

**Correction Needed:** Recalculate distances based on consistent movement rates.
