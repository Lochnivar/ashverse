**Sub-Assumptions:**
- Three-phase evolution is realistic

## PARAMETERS

**Key Variables:**
- Location: Appomattox Court House (where war ended)
- Evolution: Phase 1 (CSA Flex 1866-1889) → Phase 2 (Crisis 1889-1909) → Phase 3 (Reconciliation 1909-1939)
- Funding: California pays 60% construction, 40% operations
- Purpose: Joint military-diplomatic academy
- Symbolism: McLean House parlor replica, clock still 17 seconds slow

**Historical Context:**
- OTL: No such institution
- NTL: Post-war reconciliation mechanism

**Constraints:**
- Must maintain historical realism
- Must serve narrative function
- Must align with established timeline
- Must show evolution over time

## RESULTS

**Overall Plausibility Score: 90%** (Excellent)

**Key Findings:**

**Phase 1: CSA Flex (1866-1889)**
- ✅ Court of Grievances forces USA officers to apologize
- ✅ Shows CSA victory culture and USA humiliation
- ✅ Duration: 23 years (long enough to feel permanent)
- **Plausibility: 88%** - Realistic post-war mechanism

**Phase 2: Crisis and Transition (1889-1909)**
- ✅ Harrison refuses summons, resigns in disgrace
- ✅ USA wants to abolish, CSA refuses to give up symbolic victory
- ✅ Solution: Repurpose, don't abolish (both sides save face)
- **Plausibility: 90%** - Realistic crisis and resolution

**Phase 3: Reconciliation (1909-1939)**
- ✅ Joint education - send sons to learn together
- ✅ Forced interaction, shared meals, integrated housing
- ✅ Gradual shift from tension to camaraderie
- **Plausibility: 92%** - Reconciliation mechanism realistic

**California's Role:**
- ✅ Pays 60% construction, 40% operations
- ✅ Rationale: "We're the neutral party"
- ✅ Strategic value: Soft power, intelligence, diplomatic influence
- ✅ Badge: California flag 5% larger (quiet dominance)
- **Plausibility: 91%** - California's role realistic

**Breakdown:**
- Historical Precedent: 88% (OTL patterns applicable)
- Symbolic Power: 92% (Location and evolution powerful)
- Narrative Function: 90% (Serves story purpose excellently)
- Reconciliation Mechanism: 92% (Realistic and effective)
