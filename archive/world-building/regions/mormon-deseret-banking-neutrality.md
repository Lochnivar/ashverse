- **Status:** Sovereign theocracy, recognized in Treaty of Cincinnati Article 7
- **Economic Role:** Minimal - survival mode, self-sufficiency
- **Key:** Brigham Young's 15,000 armed men deter any invasion
- **Reality:** Both USA and CSA too exhausted to fight another Mormon War

- **Status:** Succession crisis after Brigham Young's death

- **Reality:** Deseret becomes too economically important to attack
- **1909:** Appomattox College partly funded by Deseret "neutral contribution"

### Phase 4: Continental Financial Hub (1920-1939)
- **Status:** Rich, weird, heavily armed theocracy that everyone trades with but nobody trusts
- **1919:** American Concord Treaty deposits physically held in Salt Lake City vaults
- **Economic Role:** Central banking hub for entire continent
- **Key:** Neutral broker for USA-CSA-California financial transactions
- **1929-1939:** Only major banking system that does not collapse during the Depression
- **Reality:** Economic interdependence makes Deseret untouchable
